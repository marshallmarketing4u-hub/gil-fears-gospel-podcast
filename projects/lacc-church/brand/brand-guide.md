## Color Palette

| Color | Name | Hex | RGB | Use |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Heritage Gold | LACC Gold | `#C9B77D` | `201, 183, 125` | Primary brand color, logo background, accent fields, large branded surfaces |
| Black | Black | `#000000` | `0, 0, 0` | Cross, lettering, primary type, high-contrast accents |
| Kelly Green | Oak Leaf Green | `#4CBB17` | `76, 187, 23` | Secondary accent — buttons, highlights, callouts, growth/community references |

### Color rationale

- **Heritage Gold (`#C9B77D`)** sampled directly from the logo's gold circle. A muted, warm, aged-gold rather than a shiny coin-gold. Reads as established and grounded.
- **Black (`#000000`)** — the cross, the LACC lettering, and the wordmark all sit in solid black. Holds weight against the gold without competing.
- **Kelly Green (`#4CBB17`)** — the fresh green of new oak leaves in spring. Used sparingly to reinforce the tree-of-life imagery in the logo. Pairs cleanly with the gold; do not overuse.

### Pairings and contrast

- **Black on Gold:** primary lockup. Excellent contrast. Default for logo, major headlines.
- **Gold on Black:** inverse lockup, premium feel. Use for hero sections or evening event materials.
- **Green on Black:** strong, modern. Use for CTAs and links on dark surfaces.
- **Green on Gold:** soft, low contrast. Avoid for type or buttons; acceptable for decorative accents only.
- **Black on Green:** acceptable for buttons and badges. Good legibility.

## Typography

To be defined. Open recommendations:
- **Display / Headlines:** a humanist serif with weight (e.g., Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond) — pairs with the church's traditional cross while feeling contemporary
- **Body:** a clean readable sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Source Sans 3) for screen and print

## Voice

To be defined. Likely traits:
- Warm, welcoming, community-first
- Plain language over churchy jargon
- Confident in faith without being preachy

## Imagery

To be defined. Initial direction:
- Real church members and community moments, not stock photos
- Natural light, warm tones that complement the Heritage Gold
- The tree-of-life motif from the logo can echo through photo selection (trees, growth, families, gathering)
