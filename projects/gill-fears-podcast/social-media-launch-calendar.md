## Content Production Notes

### Clipping Episodes (Daily Word)
- Pull the single most quotable or emotionally resonant 45–90 seconds
- Add subtitles — 85% of social video is watched without sound
- Format: vertical (9:16) for Reels/TikTok/Shorts, square (1:1) for Facebook feed
- Add the show logo in corner, Gil's name lower third

### Caption Formula
1. Hook — first line stops the scroll
2. Body — 2–3 sentences of context or story
3. CTA — one clear action ("Listen on Spotify," "Subscribe free," "Drop a prayer request below")

### Hashtag Strategy
Core tags to use on every post:
`#GilFears #ItsANewDay #GospelPodcast #GospelMusic #FaithDaily #ChristianPodcast #NewDayFamily #DailyDevotion #GospelCommunity #BlackChurch`
