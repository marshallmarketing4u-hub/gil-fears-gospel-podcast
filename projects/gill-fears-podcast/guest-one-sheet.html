About Gil

Gil Fears is a preacher, pastor, and storyteller whose voice has moved congregations for decades. Known for his warmth, unshakeable faith, and gift for meeting people exactly where they are, Gil has built a life around one mission: sharing the transformative power of the gospel in a way that is real, relevant, and impossible to ignore.

His daily podcast, The Gil Fears "It's A New Day" Gospel Podcast, is a fresh broadcast of faith — equal parts music, scripture, teaching, and raw conversation. Every episode is a reminder that no matter what yesterday looked like, today is a new day with God.

Gil brings the same energy to a microphone that he brings to a pulpit: conviction, compassion, and a story that draws you in and doesn't let go.

Suggested Interview Questions
  1. 1. What does "It's A New Day" mean to you personally — and why did that become the heartbeat of your show?
  2. 2. You've been preaching for decades. What made you decide to step into the podcast space now?
  3. 3. How do you keep the gospel fresh and relevant for people who feel like they've heard it all before?
  4. 4. What's the one thing you want every listener to walk away believing after they hear your show?
  5. 5. Talk to us about the New Day Family — what are you building, and who is it for?
