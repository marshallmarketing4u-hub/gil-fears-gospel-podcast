About the Show

Faith-Forward Conversations That Move You

The Gil Fears "It's A New Day" Gospel Podcast is a weekly journey into the heart of the gospel. Each episode brings raw, authentic conversations about faith, purpose, and the power of God's word in everyday life.

"Every episode is designed to meet you where you are — and take you further than you imagined."

Whether you're a lifelong believer or just beginning your spiritual journey, the Gil Fears "It's A New Day" Gospel Podcast is your weekly companion for growth, healing, and inspiration.

Gil Fears
Host & Pastor

Preacher, teacher, and storyteller — Gil Fears has spent decades sharing the transformative power of the gospel. His warmth, wisdom, and unshakeable faith come through in every episode.
