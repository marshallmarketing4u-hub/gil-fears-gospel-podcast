# Safe Haven Asset Group Brand Guide

## Brand Essence
Safe Haven Asset Group positions as a private, calm, trustworthy real estate acquisition partner.
Tone: discreet, direct, respectful, no pressure.

## Logo Placement
Use this directory for logo files:
- Primary horizontal logo: brand/logo-primary.svg
- Mark icon: brand/logo-mark.svg
- Inverse logo (for dark backgrounds): brand/logo-inverse.svg

Preferred clear space: at least the cap-height of the S in the logo wordmark on all sides.
Minimum digital width: 140px.

## Color System
Primary palette from project specifications:
- Slate: #2C3540
- Cream: #F4EFE6
- Bone: #FBF8F2
- Brass: #A8825A
- Walnut: #6E5238
- Mist: #D9D2C5
- Ink: #1F242B
- Stone: #6B7280
- Sage: #5A7A5C
- Clay: #A85A4F

Usage guidance:
- Headlines and anchors: Slate
- Body background: Cream or Bone
- Primary CTA: Brass with Bone text
- Hover emphasis: Walnut
- Form success: Sage
- Form error/warning: Clay

## Typography
Primary sans-serif: Inter
Primary serif: Source Serif 4

Usage guidance:
- Headlines, nav, labels, buttons: Inter 500 to 700
- Body copy and legal text: Source Serif 4 400 to 600

## Voice and Messaging
- Keep language clear and plain.
- Avoid hype, urgency manipulation, and aggressive sales tone.
- Prioritize privacy, clarity, and owner control.

Core message structure:
1. Private conversation
2. Practical options
3. Owner decides what happens next

## Imagery Direction
- Real properties, not staged stock feeling.
- Warm neutrals, natural light, subtle shadows.
- Avoid high-saturation, overly glossy visuals.
- Avoid obvious luxury clichés.

## CTA Language
Preferred CTA:
- Start a private conversation

Secondary CTA:
- See how it works
- Contact our team

## Legal Tone
Include this disclaimer in footer contexts:
Safe Haven Asset Group is a private real estate investment company. We are not realtors, attorneys, lenders, financial advisors, or tax professionals. Information on this site is for general purposes only and is not legal, tax, or financial advice. Property purchases are subject to inspection, title, and other due diligence.
