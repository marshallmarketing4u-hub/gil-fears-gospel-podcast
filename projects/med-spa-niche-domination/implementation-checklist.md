## 🚨 CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS

**DO THIS:**
✅ Pick one market (Long Beach/Lakewood) and dominate it first
✅ Lead with the $5K/month offer (clear value, reasonable price)
✅ Start with Tier 1 prospects (highest desperation, easiest close)
✅ Use competitor comparison as hook (every spa fears being irrelevant)
✅ Deliver exceptional Month 1 (foundation for upsells + referrals)
✅ Track everything (data drives decisions)

**DON'T DO THIS:**
❌ Try to serve all niches at once (unfocused = failure)
❌ Underprice ($1,667 tier is entry; don't lead with it)
❌ Chase hot leads (work tier by tier systematically)
❌ Over-complicate the offer (website + social + strategy = simple)
❌ Skip the strategy work (branding backend is what makes clients succeed)
❌ Forget to document results (case studies = future sales fuel)

## 📞 DISCOVERY CALL QUICK REFERENCE

**Script outline (30 min):**
- Min 0-2: Rapport building ("How long have you owned the spa?")
- Min 2-5: Pain identification ("What's your biggest acquisition challenge?")
- Min 5-10: Social proof ("Here's what I did for [similar spa]...")
- Min 10-20: Diagnosis ("Your website isn't converting; here's why...")
- Min 20-25: The offer ("90-day Market Domination Package: $5K/month")
- Min 25-30: Close ("Does this resonate? Let's lock in your spot.")

**Closing line:**
"I've got two slots opening next month. What works better: 3 consecutive monthly payments, or one upfront commitment for 10% off?"
