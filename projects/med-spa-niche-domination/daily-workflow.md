## GHL CRM Daily Command Center

Run this sequence inside GoHighLevel before noon every day.

1. Open Opportunities and sort by stage age, oldest first.
2. Move stuck leads forward or assign a next action date.
3. Complete all overdue tasks first.
4. Send follow-ups from the Engaged and Discovery stages.
5. Update opportunity values and expected close dates.
6. Add notes immediately after every call.
7. End with zero contacts missing a next step.

## 7-Day Execution Cycle

### Monday: Intelligence + Prioritization
- Score 10 prospects from Tier 1 list.
- Gather owner names, website gaps, and social gaps.
- Select top 10 for this week.
- Prepare personalized opening lines for each.

### Tuesday: Outbound Sprint 1
- Send first-touch messages to 10 prospects.
- Send 10 follow-up messages to older prospects.
- Publish one authority post on LinkedIn about med spa growth gaps.
- Confirm discovery slots for Wednesday and Thursday.
- Update GHL pipeline tags: `contacted-weekly`, `follow-up-needed`, `priority-tier1`.

### Wednesday: Sales Day
- Run 2-3 discovery calls.
- Use diagnosis-first framework.
- Send every proposal within 24 hours.
- Call back warm prospects by phone.
- Create or update each GHL opportunity with value, close date, and objection notes.

### Thursday: Outbound Sprint 2
- Send another 10 first-touch messages.
- Send 10 follow-ups.
- Record and send one 2-minute loom audit to top prospects.
- Push to fill next week discovery calendar.
- Trigger GHL workflow for no-reply contacts after 5 days.

### Friday: Delivery + Proof
- Build client assets: website section, social set, positioning doc.
- Send one progress report to each active client.
- Update before/after metrics for case studies.
- Weekly review: what converted, what stalled, what changes next week.
- Export GHL pipeline report and compare with weekly KPI targets.

### Saturday: Light Ops (Optional 60-90 min)
- Clean CRM pipeline.
- Draft next week's outreach personalization snippets.
- Review competitors for new opportunities.

### Sunday: Reset (Optional 30 min)
- Set top 3 outcomes for the next week.
- Reconfirm calendar blocks.

## Phase-Based Daily Focus

## Days 1-14 (Pipeline Build)
- Priority: prospecting and outreach volume.
- Daily minimum: 20 messages total, 1 booked call.
- Delivery work: light.

## Days 15-45 (Sales Conversion)
- Priority: discovery calls and proposals.
- Daily minimum: 1 call, 1 proposal, 10 follow-ups.
- Delivery work: moderate.

## Days 46-90 (Client Delivery + Upsell)
- Priority: fulfillment quality and measurable wins.
- Daily minimum: one client milestone moved forward.
- Sales work: maintain 30-45 minutes of follow-up daily.

## Discovery Call Day Workflow

| Step | Time | Action |
|------|------|--------|
| Pre-call prep | 20 min | Review website, social, and top 2 competitors |

| Immediate notes | 10 min | Record pain points, objections, timeline |
| Proposal draft | 30 min | Build tailored 90-day plan and pricing |
| Follow-up send | 5 min | Send proposal and booking link for close call |

## Minimum Standards

1. No zero-outreach day.
2. No discovery call without same-day follow-up.
3. No Friday without KPI review.
4. No client week without visible progress update.

## 30-Second Daily Priority Rule

If the day gets chaotic, do these three first:
1. Send 10 high-quality follow-ups.
2. Book or run one discovery call.
3. Move one client deliverable to completion.

These three actions protect pipeline, revenue, and retention.
