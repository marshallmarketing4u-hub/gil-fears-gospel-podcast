## PROSPECT QUALIFICATION RUBRIC

Use this to quickly assess fit before outreach:

**Green Light (Outreach Immediately):**
- ✅ No website or outdated website (0-3 years old)
- ✅ Instagram <500 followers or inactive
- ✅ Google Business profile but minimal reviews
- ✅ Phone number easy to find = decision-maker accessible

**Yellow Light (Research More):**
- ⚠️ Website exists but poor UX
- ⚠️ Instagram 500-2K followers, sporadic posts
- ⚠️ Reviews declining or stale
- ⚠️ No clear owner contact info

**Red Light (Skip or Different Approach):**
- ❌ Premium website + active social (already winning)
- ❌ Corporate/chain ownership (different sales process)
- ❌ Recently hired agency (unlikely to switch)
